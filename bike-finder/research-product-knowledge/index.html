Summary

Purpose: I would personally use a bike for daily commute. It would be primarily used on city streets but may also need to handle situations like muddy or uneven roads.

Prioritization: I would prioritize safety, price, and brand reputation.

Features: A lightweight and durable material like aluminum or carbon fiber. Reliable and responsive brakes for safety. For the tire, I care about the stability on varied terrains and the durability.
